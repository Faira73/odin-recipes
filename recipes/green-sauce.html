Green Sauce Grilled Cheese

Description

The green sauce as an awesome twist to the classic grilled cheese sandwich that you can also put on anything your heart desires!

Ingredients

  • For the sauce:
    • Cilantro
    • Green onions
    • A clove of garlic
    • Avocado
    • Mayonaise
    • Honey (or the sweetner of your choice)
    • Jalapeno
  • For the sandwich:
    • Sourdough bread
    • Pepper Jack cheese
    • Bacon

Steps

  1. In a blender, add the Ingredients for the sauce.
  2. Slice the bread
  3. Slice the bacon in small pieces
  4. Cook the bacon until crispy and set aside
  5. Over medium heat, melt butter and place in a slice of bread
  6. Add a slice of cheese on top of the bread, and then add your bacon, and close with the other slice
  7. Keep flipping the sandwich until the outside is crispy and the cheese is melted
  8. Open your sandwich and add the sauce
  9. Enjoy!
